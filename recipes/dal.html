Ingredients

  • 1 cup lentils (red, yellow, or green)
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, heat oil or ghee over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  3. Add chopped onion and garlic, saut√© until translucent.
  4. Add rinsed lentils, turmeric powder, and water. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-30 minutes, or until lentils are soft.
  6. Add salt to taste and stir well.
  7. Serve hot with rice or bread.
