Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 1/2 lb ground meat (chicken, pork, or beef)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour and water to form a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, combine the ground meat, onion, garlic, ginger, cabbage, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and cut into small circles (about 3 inches in diameter).
  4. Place a spoonful of the meat filling in the center of each dough circle.
  5. Fold the dough over the filling and pinch the edges to seal, forming a half-moon shape. You can pleat the edges for a traditional look.
  6. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the dough is cooked and the filling is hot.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
