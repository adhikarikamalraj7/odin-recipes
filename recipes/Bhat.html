Bhat

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  3. Add the rinsed rice, salt, and oil/ghee to the boiling water.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  5. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.
